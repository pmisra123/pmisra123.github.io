<h2 class="section-heading">About</h2>
<div class="about-grid">
  <div class="about-photo">
    <img src="/assets/images/photo.jpg" alt="Prakhar Misra">
  </div>
  <div class="about-text">
    <p>I am a Ph.D. candidate in International Political Economy at the <a href="https://www.sais-jhu.edu/">School of Advanced International Studies (SAIS)</a>, Johns Hopkins University. My fields are Political Economy, Comparative Politics, and Public Policy.</p>
    <p>My research examines the sub-national political economy of state capacity in developing countries, with a focus on the institutional processes that shape the recruitment and allocation of government personnel. I argue that meritocratic recruitment processes themselves can drive the persistence of a patronage state, and document a systematic shift toward contractual employment over permanent civil service positions in India.</p>
    <p>I use mixed-methods approaches, combining administrative data from government documents with data scraped from newspapers and e-courts databases to study recruitment policies and institutional bottlenecks.</p>
    <p>My research has been supported by grants from <a href="https://www.sais-jhu.edu/">Johns Hopkins SAIS</a>, the <a href="https://guptaklinsky.sais-jhu.edu/">Gupta-Klinsky India Institute</a>, the <a href="https://www.theihs.org/">Institute for Humane Studies</a>, the <a href="https://www.indiastudies.org/">American Institute for Indian Studies</a>, the <a href="https://apsanet.org/">American Political Science Association</a>, and <a href="https://www.sawtooth.com/">Sawtooth's Academic Grants Program</a>.</p>
    <p>I am a Research Assistant to <a href="https://www.sais-jhu.edu/directory/devesh-kapur">Professor Devesh Kapur</a> (on a development history book) and to <a href="https://www.sais-jhu.edu/directory/yuen-yuen-ang">Professor Yuen Yuen Ang</a> (on <em>The Polytunity Project</em>). Before the Ph.D., I was a policy researcher and a Teach for India Fellow, with research contributions to state committees, the Reserve Bank of India, and the World Bank's World Development Report 2025.</p>

<section class="section" id="projects">
<h2 class="section-heading">Projects</h2>

<div class="project-item">
  <div class="project-name">The 1991 Project</div>
  <div class="project-desc">A public history project documenting India's economic transformation from socialist planning to market-oriented reforms in 1991. The project revives ideas and policies from that pivotal period, targeting audiences born after 1991 who have no memory of the pre-reform economy. I authored research papers, coordinated essays, designed timelines and data visualizations, anchored lectures, and created op-eds and podcasts.</div>
  <a class="project-link" href="https://www.the1991project.com/" target="_blank">the1991project.com →</a>
</div>

<div class="project-item">
  <div class="project-name">Data Governance Network</div>
  <div class="project-desc">A two-year initiative funded by Omidyar Network to bridge differing perspectives on digital governance policy in India during a period of active regulatory discussion. The network brought together 8 organizations (think-tanks and NGOs), produced 35 research papers and policy briefs, held quarterly roundtables, and submitted findings to parliamentary committees. I led the initiative in year two, selecting research organizations, coordinating output, managing communications, and liaising with policymakers.</div>
  <a class="project-link" href="https://datagovernance.org/" target="_blank">datagovernance.org →</a>
</div>

<div class="project-item">
  <div class="project-name">Developing Infrastructure for Creating Employment (DICE)</div>
  <div class="project-desc">An IDFC Institute survey project examining the employment impact of infrastructure investment across India, funded by the Ford Foundation. The research combined nightlights data analysis, a 2,500-firm survey across 18 districts, and focus group discussions — with particular attention to peri-urban regions. The final report was launched by Minister Nitin Gadkari.</div>
  <a class="project-link" href="https://www.idfcinstitute.org/projects/transitions/developing-infrastructure-for-creating-employment-dice/" target="_blank">IDFC Institute →</a>
</div>
</section>
